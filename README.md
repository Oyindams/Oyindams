## About

I am a final year Computer Science student at McPherson University focused on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. I build intelligent systems that solve real-world problems across healthcare, agriculture, finance, and emerging markets.

I have industry experience in both networking and software development. I interned as a Network Engineer at Huawei (a telecommunications company) and Teltwine (a network services provider), where I gained practical knowledge of radio frequency systems and mobile network technologies including 2G, 3G, and 4G. I also worked with geospatial and network planning tools such as QGIS and Google Earth Pro.

In addition, I have experience as a Frontend Developer at a Japanese company, where I worked with technologies including HTML, CSS, Vue.js, and React to build and improve user-facing applications.

Currently, I am focused on developing AI and machine learning projects, including an automated loan system that assesses creditworthiness before approval. I am also exploring automation systems as part of my growing interest in intelligent, scalable solutions.
